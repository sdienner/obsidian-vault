# 3-Year Vision

> What does success look like by April 2029?

## Career & Leadership

- Known as a leader who develops other leaders — people on my team take ownership of delivery without me in the loop
- Have transitioned from operational executor (releases, MFP management, day-to-day priority calls) to internal product strategist and community builder
- Scope of impact extends beyond my direct team to the broader company through the Code Collective

## Engineering Impact

- Cargas Code Collective is active and self-sustaining — a steering committee is established, developers across the company contribute to internal tools on allocated time, and the idea-to-implementation pipeline is real
- A portfolio of internal applications exists that people actually use, built by identifying and solving genuine company needs across departments
- AI automation and internal tooling (AskHub, Document Extraction, ai.cargas.com) are running and maintained by the team, not dependent on me

## Personal

- Spending the majority of my time on discovery, strategy, and enablement — identifying internal needs, sponsoring projects, unblocking people
- Not the bottleneck for day-to-day operational tasks (releases, MFP, priority calls)
- Feel like I'm building something, not just maintaining it
