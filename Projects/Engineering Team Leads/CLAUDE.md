# Project: Engineering Team Leads

## Overview
Establishing Team Leader positions on the Energy Product Development team to distribute leadership responsibilities, improve team support through mentor check-ins, and create a leadership partnership for process improvement and performance awareness.

## Status
- **Phase:** Planning
- **Progress:** 15%
- **Started:** 2026-05-07
- **Target:** TBD (pending Fred conversation)

## Goals
- Create 2-3 Team Leader roles (senior IC, no direct reports) modeled on existing Cargas Team Leader template
- Improve team support through regular mentor check-ins (not management 1:1s)
- Establish a leadership group that meets regularly to assess team health, engineering processes, and priorities
- Get structured review input without fully delegating performance management
- Develop leadership pipeline for the team
