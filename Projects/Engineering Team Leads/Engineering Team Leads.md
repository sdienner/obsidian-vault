# Engineering Team Leads

## The Problem

11 direct reports is too many for one manager to support well. Monthly 1:1s need to increase in frequency, but going biweekly solo would mean ~22 hrs/month — not sustainable. Beyond time, there's a need for more perspectives on team health, process improvement, and performance awareness.

## The Model

**Team Leader = senior IC with expanded scope, no direct reports.**

Modeled on existing Cargas Team Leader roles (Customer Support, Intacct) which are explicitly individual contributor positions. This version expands scope to include mentor check-ins and performance review input.

### What Team Leaders Do
- Run biweekly **mentor check-ins** with assigned team members (not management 1:1s — growth, blockers, connection)
- Provide **structured observations** for performance reviews (specific examples of impact, growth, collaboration)
- Meet **regularly as a leadership group** with Scott to assess team health, evaluate engineering processes, align on priorities, identify what's working and what needs focus
- Drive **process improvements** — identify inefficiencies, optimize workflows, implement best practices
- Serve as **first point of escalation** for team questions and blockers
- Maintain their full SE responsibilities

### What Team Leaders Don't Do
- No direct reports — reporting lines unchanged
- No career 1:1s — Scott retains monthly career conversations with all 11
- No review ownership — Scott writes, owns, and delivers all performance reviews
- No hire/fire/PIP authority
- No PTO approval or work assignment authority

### Structure
- Outside the SE leveling framework (lateral role, not a promotion on the technical ladder)
- Each TL paired with ~4 team members for mentor check-ins
- Weekly leadership sync (Scott + TLs) — 30 min, first quarter; evaluate biweekly after

## Job Description Draft

**Job Title:** Team Leader, Product Software Engineering
**Business Unit / Department:** Energy / Product Development
**Reports to:** Software Engineering Manager
**Job Level / Grade:** TBD
**FLSA Classification:** Exempt
**Work Type:** Hybrid

### Job Summary
The Team Leader is a senior individual contributor within the Energy Product Software Engineering team who provides technical expertise and drives process improvements across the team. This role supports team effectiveness by mentoring team members, identifying inefficiencies, and contributing to a collaborative team culture. While not a people manager, the Team Leader serves as a trusted resource for peers and a partner to the Engineering Manager in maintaining team health, performance awareness, and continuous improvement.

### Primary Responsibilities
1. Consistently demonstrate Cargas Core Values.
2. Serve as a technical leader within the Product Software Engineering team while fulfilling all responsibilities of a Product Software Engineer.
3. Conduct regular mentor check-ins with assigned team members to support their growth, surface blockers, and maintain team connection.
4. Provide structured observations and input to the Engineering Manager for use in performance reviews, including specific examples of impact, growth, and collaboration.
5. Lead process improvement initiatives by identifying inefficiencies, optimizing workflows, and implementing best practices.
6. Participate in leadership discussions to help shape team-wide goals, processes, and standards.
7. Meet regularly with the Engineering Manager and fellow Team Leader(s) as a leadership group to assess team health, evaluate engineering processes, align on priorities, and identify what's working well and where the team should focus next.
8. Stay aware of team workload, skillsets, and capacity; make recommendations on task distribution.
9. Act as a subject matter expert by sharing knowledge and supporting technical problem-solving across the team.
10. Serve as a first point of escalation for team questions and blockers before they reach the Engineering Manager.
11. Contribute to a positive, high-trust team culture through consistent communication and collaboration.

### Secondary Responsibilities
1. Support onboarding of new team members.
2. Other duties as assigned.

### Minimum Qualifications
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (or equivalent combination of education and experience)
- **Experience:** 5 years of experience in software development
- **Skills:**
    - Demonstrated technical expertise in team's technology stack
    - Strong mentoring and coaching ability
    - Effective written and verbal communication
    - Ability to identify process improvements and drive them to completion
    - Collaborative mindset with ability to work across roles (PMs, QA, Support)

### Preferred Qualifications
- Experience providing technical leadership or mentorship in a team setting
- Familiarity with Cargas Energy products and business domain
- Experience with performance feedback or peer review processes

### Leadership Responsibilities
This position is an individual contributor. The Team Leader does not have direct reports but is expected to actively mentor peers and contribute to the Engineering Manager's understanding of team performance and dynamics.
