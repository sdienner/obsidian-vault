# Product Software Engineer Role Progression Framework

Cargas Energy product engineering ladder. P1–P5. Used for performance reviews, promotion decisions, and career development conversations.

---

## P1 — Product Software Engineer

**Job Summary**
Develops foundational software engineering skills while contributing to product development under guidance. Focuses on learning core technologies, implementing well-defined features, and resolving straightforward technical issues. Works closely with senior team members to understand product requirements and deliver quality code that meets established standards.

**Impact / Scope**
Responsibility focused on individual assignments and tasks within a limited number of subsystems. Follows defined team processes to complete assigned work.

**Expertise**
Actively learns and adopts the team's technologies and tools. Follows established processes to complete well-defined tasks on a limited number of subsystems with guidance.

**Functional Competencies**

| Area | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| Technical Execution & Quality | Actively learning the technologies the product is built with. Writes clean, well-tested code for clearly defined tasks. Participates in code reviews to learn from feedback. |
| Industry & Product Acumen | Actively learning the basic functions of the product and working towards understanding customers. |
| Engineering & System Design | Implements designs provided by other engineers, asking clarifying questions to ensure understanding. |
| Leadership & Influence | Takes ownership of individual tasks and their impact on a subsystem. |
| Operational Excellence | Follows team processes for standard procedures (builds, releases, commits, etc.) |
| Collaboration | Works with PMs, designers, and QA to develop new features. Works with product/technical support to troubleshoot production issues. |

**Required Qualifications**
- Education: Bachelor's in CS, Software Engineering, or related field
- Experience: Entry-level
- Skills: SQL, Web Dev Tools, Git
- Preferred: Jira

**Promotion Criteria**
N/A — hired into this role.

## P2 — Product Software Engineer II

**Job Summary**
Independently develops and maintains product features with moderate complexity. Takes ownership of specific product modules, resolves technical issues through systematic troubleshooting and root cause analysis. Collaborates effectively with QA and support. Contributes to technical decisions within assigned areas.

**Impact / Scope**
Responsibility for features within specific product modules where they have deep expertise. Responsibility extends to the success of the whole team through proactive support and enforcement of team processes.

**Expertise**
Is the go-to person for one or more technologies and has deep expertise in specific product modules. Proactively supports other team members and enforces team processes, making an impact on the whole team's success.

**Functional Competencies**

| Area | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| Technical Execution & Quality | Has learned the most important technologies the product is built with. Independently implements and delivers moderately complex features and bug fixes with high quality. Provides thoughtful feedback in code reviews. |
| Industry & Product Acumen | Develops deep expertise in a specific module. Can troubleshoot complex customer issues within that area. |
| Engineering & System Design | Designs individual components or features within an existing, well-defined architecture. Continues to implement designs provided by others for larger projects. |
| Leadership & Influence | Proactively supports other team members to help them succeed. Is a reliable team player. |
| Operational Excellence | Helps enforce team processes, ensuring team members understand the benefits of process and consequences of non-adherence. Adept at optimizing performance to prevent future issues. |
| Collaboration | Works with PMs and senior engineers to provide technical estimates. Provides guidance to QA on what to include in test plans. |

**Required Qualifications**
- Education: Bachelor's in CS, Software Engineering, or related field
- Experience: Minimum 2 years

**Promotion Criteria**
Demonstrates P2 functional competencies for 6 months.

---

## P3 — Product Software Engineer III

**Job Summary**
Designs and implements complex product features that directly impact customer experience and business outcomes. Leads technical decision-making for significant product modules. Proactively identifies and resolves challenging technical issues while mentoring junior engineers. Drives product innovation through deep technical expertise.

**Impact / Scope**
Responsibility extends to implementing features that span multiple product modules and influencing other teams. Includes mentoring other engineers and challenging team processes to drive improvements.

**Expertise**
Researches and introduces new technologies to the team. Understands how different system components interact and can implement features that span multiple modules. Mentors others, challenges team processes, and focuses on impacting the larger Energy team.

**Functional Competencies**

| Area | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| Technical Execution & Quality | Has learned all important technologies and mastered at least one to a point where they can mentor the team. Implements complex features with a focus on quality, scalability, and maintainability. Improves codebase health through refactoring and mentoring on best practices. |
| Industry & Product Acumen | Understands how multiple parts of the product integrate to meet broader business goals. Collaborates effectively with PMs to translate ambiguous requirements into technical solutions. |
| Engineering & System Design | Designs and implements features that may span multiple system modules. |
| Leadership & Influence | Assists and advises other engineers to accelerate their career growth. Makes an impact on the engineering and support teams through specialized knowledge, contributions, and positive attitude. |
| Operational Excellence | Contributes to existing processes and looks for improvements (automating workflows, improving release management). Takes ownership of complex problems they may not have specialized knowledge for. Assists support team in difficult situations with practical solutions. |
| Collaboration | Works with PMs to provide technical designs and reasonable estimates independently. Provides critical technical feedback during discovery to shape feature trajectory. |

**Required Qualifications**
- Education: Bachelor's in CS, Software Engineering, or related field
- Experience: Minimum 5 years

**Promotion Criteria**
Demonstrates P3 functional competencies for 6 months.

## P4 — Product Software Engineer IV

**Job Summary**
Architects and delivers foundational product capabilities that enable significant business value and competitive advantage. Drives technical strategy across multiple product areas, making critical technology decisions that impact the entire engineering organization. Solves the most complex technical challenges while establishing engineering standards and best practices.

**Impact / Scope**
Holds responsibility for the technical architecture of products and systems, predicting how changes will impact the entire system. Responsible for coordinating team members on projects and adjusting team processes, with influence impacting the entire tech organization.

**Expertise**
Possesses deep knowledge of the entire technology stack and comprehends the full technical and business architecture. Coordinates team members, moderates discussions, adjusts processes based on feedback, and makes a technical impact felt across the company. Deeply understands the challenges and limitations of Product Management and Design and seeks to accelerate their work.

**Functional Competencies**

| Area | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| Technical Execution & Quality | Has mastered the entire technology stack and resolves the most complex technical issues, including difficult performance problems. Sets the standard for technical excellence and code quality. |
| Industry & Product Acumen | Fully comprehends the business architecture and customer workflows of many modules. Provides invaluable technical insights during discovery sessions that shape feature definitions. |
| Engineering & System Design | Leads the technical design of large-scale projects and can predict how changes will impact the entire system. Focuses on building robust and expansible systems while balancing timing constraints for go-to-market decisions. |
| Leadership & Influence | Coaches team members on complex projects, moderates technical discussions, and provides effective feedback. Actively invests in the career growth, performance, and well-being of team members. |
| Operational Excellence | Suggests new team processes based on feedback and guides the team through change management. Leads the response to critical production incidents and performance rescue situations. |
| Collaboration | Makes an impact that extends beyond their immediate team, frequently collaborating with PM, design, and support. Interacts directly with customers when escalated; instills confidence through rational, empathetic, clear communication. |

**Required Qualifications**
- Education: Bachelor's in CS, Software Engineering, or related field
- Experience: Minimum 8 years
- Skills: SQL Mastery, Web Dev Mastery, IT Mastery

**Promotion Criteria**
Demonstrates P4 functional competencies for 6 months.

---

## P5 — Product Software Engineer V

**Job Summary**
Defines and drives the technical vision for foundational product capabilities enabling transformational business outcomes. Establishes engineering standards, architectural patterns, and technology strategies that influence the entire engineering organization. Creates reusable frameworks and tools that amplify the productivity of all engineering teams. Serves as technical advisor to senior leadership.

**Impact / Scope**
Responsible for designing and creating new technologies widely used across the organization. Envisions the future of product capabilities and shapes the technical direction of the product.

**Expertise**
Designs and creates new technologies used by internal or external teams. Envisions future product capabilities aligned with industry trends and customer needs. Defines processes for the team, fosters career growth through coaching, and has an impact on the wider Cargas tech community.

**Functional Competencies**

| Area | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| Technical Execution & Quality | Architects and implements foundational code, frameworks, or tools that raise the bar for quality and productivity across the entire engineering organization. |
| Industry & Product Acumen | Understands the product as well as a Product Manager and can act as one if needed. Envisions future product capabilities aligned with market trends and strategic business initiatives. |
| Engineering & System Design | Defines the long-term technical architecture and vision for major parts of the product. |
| Leadership & Influence | Actively invests in the career growth, performance, and well-being of team members, fostering a positive and high-performing culture. Makes an impact on the entire tech organization through architectural decisions, strategic input, and mentorship. Makes an impact on the broader tech community (open-source, public speaking, writing). |
| Operational Excellence | Assists in defining the organization's processes for building, deploying, and maintaining resilient and scalable systems. Creates new technologies and patterns to improve operational excellence company-wide. |
| Collaboration | Works with the other leaders of the Energy team to ensure the team has tools and technology needed for customers' experience. Works with business unit technical teams to ensure functionality provides value. |

**Required Qualifications**
- Education: Bachelor's in CS, Software Engineering, or related field
- Experience: Minimum 12 years

**Promotion Criteria**
Demonstrates P5 functional competencies for 1 year.
